Piano

The piano repertoire is one of the largest and richest in classical music. The act of performing a whole work or recital entirely on one’s own is a very demanding yet empowering and somewhat magical experience. For myself, I believe it is important to perform music of today with the same enthusiasm as we perform music of the past. With this in mind, most of my solo concerts are a mixture of contemporary and ‘classical’ repertoire.
