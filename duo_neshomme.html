Duo נשמה (Neshomme)

“Between hope and despair”

Duo Neshomme

Clarinet-Piano duo with Mor Levin, focusing on repertoire that reflects a part of the Jewish-European history. Our upcoming program, "Between hope and despair" is a concert combining music and paintings of Mieczyslaw Weinberg, Paul Ben-Haim, Mario Castelnuovo-Tedesco en Arnold Schoenberg.
