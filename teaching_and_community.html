Teaching and Community

Music is for sharing both on and off stage.

Teaching

Anne teaches classical piano at the Cultural Centre of the Technical University in Delft and privately in Hilversum.

A masters graduate from the Conservatory of Amsterdam and with many years of experience, Anne offers lessons to students of all ages and ability, from beginners to advanced. Currently, Anne is primarily teaching adults and older teenagers.

Anne teaches in English, Dutch, or Russian. For more information, or to schedule a free trial lesson, please get in touch.

In the Community

Aside from her professional commitments, Anne enjoys working with Amateurs who have a love and passion for music.

Anne has also done a number of tours for Stichting Musicians Worldwide which organise concerts in nursing homes throughout the Netherlands, bringing music to the ill and elderly that otherwise would not be able to attend a concert.
