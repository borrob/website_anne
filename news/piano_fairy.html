Visit from the piano fairy!

I am very grateful and honoured to have a wonderful Yamaha C3 on loan to me from the Dutch Musical Instruments Foundation. This instrument was restored by the wonderful people at Andreisson’s piano shop and I swear it’s the most beautiful yamaha I have ever played. Practise just got a whole lot better :)

This piano is part of the 'The Willem G. Vogelaar collection'.
