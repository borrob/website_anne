Research 🧐

I have always searched for new ways to perform and bring new musical experiences to the public. In recent years, this has led to the exploration of the intersection between piano playing and live coding performance practices. At the core of the research is my CodeKlavier project together with Felipe Ignacio Noriega. With the CodeKlavier, the pianist is able to code by playing the piano. In 2015 I started the docARTES doctoral program at Orpheus Institute through Leiden University and the Conservatorium of Amsterdam. However, this doctoral trajectory is currently on (long term) hold.

From 2021-2022, I am the Creative Coding Utrecht research delegate for the “On the Fly” project funded by the Creative European Program. Whilst I have not completed my thesis, I have co-written a number of publications. Feedback is always welcome!
