“The piano is a monster that screams when you touch its teeth”

Andres Segovia

Anne Veinberg is an Australian pianist based in the Netherlands. Anne is passionate about music of and for today. She regularly collaborates with composers, actors and technologists to develop new works and musical experiences. Together with Felipe Ignacio Noriega, Anne is founder and co-creator of the CodeKlavier: a project which explores how one can live code with the piano. Anne is a member of Ensemble Scala for microtonal music, of Apituley’s Locomotive Band for music theatre productions and of Keyboard Extravaganza: a duo with Ere Lievonen playing an array of keyboard instruments.Anne also joins Felipe Ignacio Noriega to form Off<>zz - a live coding and piano duo.

Anne regularly performs in the Netherlands and internationally in concerts, conferences and other happenings. She has performed at many key festivals for contemporary music including Gaudeamus Music, Rewire, La Escucha Errante and Sonorities. Anne also enjoys bringing music outside of the concert hall and recently completed a tour of CodeKlavier’s ARquatic with Felipe Ignacio Noriega through the Botanical Gardens of the Netherlands. ARquatic was awarded the Joke ‘t Hart prijs in 2022.

Besides performing, Anne teaches piano at TU Delft’s X program. She is also developing as an artistic director and producer having joined the directors team at Stichting Toets des Tijds in 2018 and organising a tour for CodeKlavier’s ARquatic in the summer of 2021 and late summer of 2022.
