Teaching

Australian pianist, Anne Veinberg, is a committed teacher in the Hilversum/Amsterdam area.

A masters graduate from the Conservatory of Amsterdam and with many years of experience, Anne offers lessons to students of all ages and ability, from beginners to advanced.

Anne teaches in English, Russian or Dutch and lessons take place at the student's home or at a mutually convenient location. For more information, or to schedule a free trial lesson, please get in touch.
